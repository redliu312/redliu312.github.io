## About 

Recently I wrote some python scripts to test our firmware.
These scripts are extensions from the packages I wrote,the packages  send sg_ses commands to the SCSI expanders via sg3_utils.
I found I wrote some of these scripts with the same patterns like,download something ,check version and record the result.
The real problems to slow my speed to complete these stuff are  primarily on my typing speed. I met the same problem on writing solutions to the Kattis online judge.I realize if I will work in this field for the next some years I should speed up my typing speed and improve the accuracy. I found a [free training course](http://www.typingtrainer.com/) , so I decide to record how fast I can type in this article.

**update**

My collegue told me there is a [website](http://play.typeracer.com/) to play typing racing. 
very interesting to improve the typing speed.
